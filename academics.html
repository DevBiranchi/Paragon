Academics

The school curriculum is designed to empower students and enhance the necessary skills for academic success. From the start, students are motivated to become independent learners. Our curriculum fosters the growth of each student's learning and personal development. We prioritize reading as a crucial academic skill and resilience as a fundamental behavior. In order to thrive in an ever-evolving world, young individuals require confidence, resilience, and abundant chances to take initiative.

Grade 1 - 5

From the very beginning, Paragon Public School instills a sense of independence in its students, encouraging them to become self-directed learners. The curriculum is designed to support each student's growth and development, both academically and personally. Reading is given great importance as an essential academic skill, while resilience is fostered as a valuable behavioral trait.

The Primary School at Paragon serves as a crucial link between the early years' focus on creativity and exploration, and the later years' demand for academic rigor. It offers a wide range of extra- and co-curricular activities to engage students. Teachers in the primary grades prioritize the socio-emotional well-being of their students right from the planning stage, ensuring a safe and inclusive environment. They collaborate with other school staff and parents whenever necessary to create a supportive community for the students.

Grade 6 - 10

At Paragon, our middle school students thrive in a tight-knit community of eager learners. They enjoy a stimulating curriculum, diverse classes, and educational field trips. Hands-on learning opportunities allow them to apply their knowledge in real-life situations. Additionally, they can participate in various extracurricular activities like sports, theater, arts, and more.

The Secondary School at Paragon maintains high academic standards and encourages skill development beyond the classroom. Our modern Science and STEAM labs enable students to turn theory into practice. By offering these resources, we empower students to explore and apply their knowledge, preparing them for future academic and professional pursuits. Our Secondary school program aims to provide a strong foundation for future success.

A private educational institution that aims to develop skilled manpower for the nation.
