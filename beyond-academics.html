Beyond Academics

The school curriculum is designed to empower students and enhance the necessary skills for academic success. From the start, students are motivated to become independent learners. Our curriculum fosters the growth of each student's learning and personal development. We prioritize reading as a crucial academic skill and resilience as a fundamental behavior. In order to thrive in an ever-evolving world, young individuals require confidence, resilience, and abundant chances to take initiative.

Feild Trips

Students of all ages are provided feild trips across the country. These educational excursions offer students a unique opportunity to broaden their horizons beyond the confines of the classroom, allowing them to immerse themselves in real-world experiences that complement their academic studies. Field trips foster a hands-on learning environment, encouraging students to engage with the subjects they are studying in a more tangible way.

Whether exploring historical landmarks, scientific institutions, or cultural hubs, these trips provide a dynamic and interactive platform for students to connect theoretical knowledge with practical applications. Additionally, exposure to different regions and diverse cultures fosters a sense of global awareness and appreciation, contributing to the development of well-rounded individuals.

Music

The school has organized music and dance classes for all the students to sharpen their skills in the feild they like. Complementing traditional academic pursuits, the school has taken a proactive approach to nurture students' artistic talents by organizing music and dance classes. These specialized classes provide a dedicated space for students to explore and enhance their skills in the creative fields they are passionate about.

Whether it's mastering a musical instrument, refining dance techniques, or discovering the intricacies of composition, students are given the tools and guidance to express themselves artistically. Beyond skill development, these classes foster a sense of creativity, self-expression, and discipline, contributing to the holistic development of each student. The initiative not only acknowledges the importance of a well-rounded education but also recognizes the diverse talents that students bring to the school community.

Sports

Students participate in different sport such as basketball, futsal, and table-tennis. Emphasizing the significance of physical fitness and fostering a spirit of healthy competition, the school encourages students to engage in a variety of sports, including basketball, futsal, and table tennis. The diverse range of sporting activities not only promotes physical well-being but also instills valuable life skills such as teamwork, leadership, and resilience.

Through participation in these sports, students develop a sense of camaraderie and sportsmanship, creating a supportive community within the school. These sporting endeavors go beyond the physical aspect, contributing to the overall personal growth of students by enhancing their mental discipline and fostering a commitment to a healthy, active lifestyle.
